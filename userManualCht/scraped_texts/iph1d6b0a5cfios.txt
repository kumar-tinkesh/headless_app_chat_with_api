Duplicate and copy photos and videos on iPhone
In the Photos app on iPhone, you can duplicate a photo or video while preserving the original version. You can also copy a photo and then paste it into another document such as an email, text message, or presentation.
Duplicate a photo or video
Go to the Photos app on your iPhone.
Open a photo or video, then tap .
Tap Duplicate.
A duplicate copy appears next to the original in your library.
Copy a photo
Go to the Photos app on your iPhone.
Open a photo, then tap .
Tap Copy, then paste the photo into another document.
Copy multiple photos or videos
Go to the Photos app on your iPhone.
Swipe up to view your library.
Tap Select, then tap the thumbnails you want to copy.
Tap , then tap Copy.
Paste the copies into another document.
