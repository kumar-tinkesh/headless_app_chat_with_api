iPhone User Guide
Rearrange the icons on CarPlay Home
When you’re not operating your vehicle, open Settings on your iPhone, go to General > CarPlay, select your vehicle, then tap Customize.
Do one of the following:
Move an icon: Drag up or down in the list.
Remove an icon: Tap next to the icon, then tap Remove.
Only icons that have next to them can be removed.
Add an icon back to CarPlay: Below More Apps at the bottom of the page, tap next to the icon.
Your icon changes appear on CarPlay Home the next time you connect to CarPlay.
