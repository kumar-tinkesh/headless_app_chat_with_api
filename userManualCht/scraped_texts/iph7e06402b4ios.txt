Take panoramic photos with your iPhone camera
Use your iPhone camera to take a panoramic photo of your surroundings in Pano mode .
Open Camera on your iPhone.
Choose Pano mode.
Tap the Shutter button or click the Camera Control ( on supported models ).
Pan slowly in the direction of the arrow, keeping it on the center line.
To finish, tap the Shutter button again or click the Camera Control ( on supported models ).
Tap the arrow to pan in the opposite direction. To pan vertically, rotate iPhone to landscape orientation. You can reverse the direction of a vertical pan, too.
