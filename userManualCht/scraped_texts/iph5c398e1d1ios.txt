See a webpage’s highlights in Safari on iPhone
You can learn more about the current webpage by looking at Highlights. Highlights share helpful information, such as directions, summaries, and links to additional details about people, music, movies, and TV shows.
Note: Highlights aren’t available in all countries or regions.
