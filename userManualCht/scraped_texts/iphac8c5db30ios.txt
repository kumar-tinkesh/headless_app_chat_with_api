Get notified about car horns and sirens in CarPlay
With Sound Recognition in CarPlay, you can have iPhone continuously listen for car horns and sirens, and recognize them using on-device intelligence. A notification appears on the CarPlay touchscreen when these sounds are detected.
Note: Sound Recognition in CarPlay is available with supported iPhone models . In CarPlay, go to Settings > Accessibility.
Tap Sound Recognition, then turn on Sound Recognition.
Turn on the sounds you want to be notified about.
