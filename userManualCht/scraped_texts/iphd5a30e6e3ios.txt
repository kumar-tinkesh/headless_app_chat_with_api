iPhone User Guide
Register as an organ donor in Health on iPhone (U.S. only)
You can register to be an organ, eye, or tissue donor with Donate Life America. If you later change your decision, you can remove your registration. Your decision to donate is accessible to others in your Medical ID.
Learn about organ donation
Go to the Health app on your iPhone.
Tap your picture or initials at the top right.
If you don’t see your picture or initials, tap Summary or Browse at the bottom of the screen, then scroll to the top of the screen.
Tap Organ Donation, then tap Learn More for an overview of organ donation and Donate Life America.
Register with Donate Life America
Go to the Health app on your iPhone.
Tap your picture or initials at the top right.
If you don’t see your picture or initials, tap Summary or Browse at the bottom of the screen, then scroll to the top of the screen.
Tap Sign Up with Donate Life.
To later change your donor information or remove your registration, tap your picture or initials, tap Organ Donation, then tap Edit Donor Registration.
