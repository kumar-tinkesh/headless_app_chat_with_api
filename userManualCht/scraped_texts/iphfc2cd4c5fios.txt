iPhone User Guide
Make and receive phone calls with CarPlay
Use CarPlay to make phone calls and listen to voicemail from your iPhone.
Siri: Say something like: “Call Eliza.” Learn how to use Siri .
Or you can use your vehicle’s built-in controls to help make a call.
Open Phone in CarPlay, then select an option.
Note: If you’re viewing CarPlay Dashboard and Phone doesn’t appear in the recent apps list on the left, tap to see pages of all of your CarPlay apps, including Phone.
