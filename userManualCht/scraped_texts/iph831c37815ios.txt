Export a Voice Memos recording to Files on iPhone
Any recording can be exported from Voice Memos to the Files app on your iPhone. Layered recordings are in QTA (QuickTime Audio) format. One-layer recordings are in .m4a format.
Go to the Voice Memos app (in the Utilities folder) on your iPhone.
Tap the recording you want to export, tap , then tap .
Scroll down, tap Save to Files, navigate to the location where you want to save the recording, then tap Save.
