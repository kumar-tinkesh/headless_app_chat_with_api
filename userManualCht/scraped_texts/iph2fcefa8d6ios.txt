iPhone User Guide
Learn more about your medications in Health on iPhone (U.S. only)
You can learn more about the medications you add to your medications list.
Learn about a medication
You can learn more about what a medication is used for, how it works, potential side effects, and how to pronounce it.
Go to the Health app on your iPhone.
Tap Browse at the bottom right, then tap Medications.
Tap the medication (in the list of your medications), then scroll down.
Learn about interactions between your medications
Go to the Health app on your iPhone.
Tap Browse at the bottom right, then tap Medications.
Tap Drug Interactions.
To learn whether interaction factors like alcohol might interact with your medications, tap Edit (in the Interaction Factors area), select the factors that apply, then tap Done.
To learn more about an interaction, tap it.
