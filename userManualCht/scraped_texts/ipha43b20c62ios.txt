iPhone User Guide
Book rides in Maps on iPhone
You can request a ride with a compatible ridesharing app. If you don’t have one installed, you can see which ones are available from the App Store.
Note: Ridesharing is available in select areas. Features vary by country and region.
Find a ride
Go to the Maps app on your iPhone.
Search for the place you want to go, then tap (at the right).
Tap Open to see the option in the ridesharing app, then book your ride in that app.
Stop sharing your location with a ridesharing app
To estimate wait times and fares, Maps may share your location with ridesharing apps. You can stop sharing your location with ridesharing apps.
Go to Settings > Apps > Maps.
Tap Ride Booking, then turn off any app.
