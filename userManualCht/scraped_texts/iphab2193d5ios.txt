iPhone User Guide
Read PDF documents in the Books app on iPhone
You can open and save PDFs in the Books app that you receive in Mail, Messages, or other apps.
Open a PDF you receive in another app in Books
Tap the PDF attachment to open it.
Tap , then tap Books from the list options.
Share or print a PDF
Go to the Books app on your iPhone.
Tap Library, then tap below the PDF.
Tap , then choose a share option such as AirDrop, Mail, or Messages, or tap Print.
Mark up a PDF in Books
Go to the Books app on your iPhone.
Open the PDF.
Tap the page, then tap at the top of the screen to use the drawing and annotation tools.
