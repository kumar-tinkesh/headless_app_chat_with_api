iPhone User Guide
Save stories in News on iPhone
You can save news stories to read later. Apple News+ subscribers can read saved stories in Offline Mode .
Save a story
Go to the News app on your iPhone.
When reading a story, tap , then tap Save Story.
Read a saved story
Go to the News app on your iPhone.
Tap Following, tap Saved Stories, then tap the story.
Delete a saved story
Go to the News app on your iPhone.
Tap Following, tap Saved Stories, then do one of the following:
Delete one story: Swipe the story left.
Remove all saved stories: Tap Clear.
