Find settings on iPhone
In the Settings app , you can search for iPhone settings you want to change, such as your passcode, notification sounds, and more.
Tap Settings on the Home Screen (or in App Library ).
Swipe down to reveal the search field, tap the search field, enter a term—“volume,” for example—then tap a setting.
Tip: You can also search for settings from the Home Screen or Lock Screen.
