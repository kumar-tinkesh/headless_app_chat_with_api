Change your voicemail greeting and settings on iPhone
You can change your voicemail greeting, password, and alert sounds.
Go to the Phone app on your iPhone.
Do any of the following to change your voicemail settings:
Change your greeting: Tap Voicemail, tap Greeting, tap Custom, then tap Record (not supported by all carriers or in all countries and regions).
Change your voicemail password: Go to Settings > Apps > Phone > Change Voicemail Password, then enter the new password.
If you forgot your voicemail password, contact your wireless carrier.
Change the alert for new voicemail: Go to Settings > Sounds & Haptics, then tap New Voicemail.
Note: To change your Contact Photo & Poster, see Add or edit your photo and poster .
