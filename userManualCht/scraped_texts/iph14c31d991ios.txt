iPhone User Guide
See previous calculations in Calculator on iPhone
Keep track of previous calculations in the basic or scientific calculators, and copy results from them to use in Calculator or other apps.
Go to the Calculator app on your iPhone.
Tap , then select Basic or Scientific.
Tap , then do any of the following:
Reload the calculation: Tap the listing. The calculation appears where you left off earlier.
Copy the answer: Touch and hold the listing, then tap Copy Expression or Copy Result.
Delete the calculation: Touch and hold the listing, then tap Delete.
