Ultra Wideband information
Ultra Wideband is available on all iPhone 11, iPhone 12, iPhone 13, iPhone 14, iPhone 15, and iPhone 16 models, and availability varies by region.
Ultra Wideband must be turned off when onboard aircraft, ships, and other prohibited regions by turning on airplane mode. To turn on airplane mode, open Control Center , then tap . You can also turn airplane mode on or off in Settings . When airplane mode is on, appears in the status bar.
Australia: Ultra Wideband transmitters must not be operated within a nominated distance from specified Australian radio-astronomy sites. For further information about nominated distance, please refer to the Radiocommunications (Low Interference Potential Devices) Class License 2015 published by the Australian Communications and Media Authority.
