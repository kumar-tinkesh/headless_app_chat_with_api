iPhone User Guide
Search your Freeform boards on iPhone
You can search all your Freeform boards for text in handwritten text, sticky notes, text boxes, shapes, and filenames.
Go to the Freeform app on your iPhone.
Do any of the following:
Search within a board: Tap the board’s name or (near the top-right corner), then tap Find.
Search all boards: Tap the search bar near the top of the All Boards screen. Any board that contains the text appears in the search results.
(If you’re in a board, tap to access Search in All Boards.)
