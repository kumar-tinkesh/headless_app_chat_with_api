Use VoiceOver on iPhone with a pointer device
If you use a pointer device with iPhone, you can adjust how it works with VoiceOver.
Go to Settings > AccessibilityÂ > VoiceOver.
Below Pointer Control, tap any of the following:
Pointer: Set the pointer to ignore, follow, or move the VoiceOver cursor.
Speak Under Pointer: You can adjust the delay to speak the item under the pointer.
