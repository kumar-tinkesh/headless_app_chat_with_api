iPhone User Guide
Look around places in Maps on iPhone
You can look around some places with 360-degree panoramic views.
Go to the Maps app on your iPhone.
In select cities, tap near the bottom of a map.
To change the view, do any of the following:
Pan: Drag a finger left or right on the scene.
Move forward: Tap the scene.
Zoom in or out: Pinch open or closed.
View another point of interest: Switch from full-screen view, then tap elsewhere on the map, or drag the map.
Switch to or from full-screen view: Tap or .
Remove labels in full-screen view: Swipe up from the place name near the bottom of the screen, then tap Hide Labels .
When finished, tap Done.
Look Around is available in select cities. See the iOS and iPadOS Feature Availability website .
