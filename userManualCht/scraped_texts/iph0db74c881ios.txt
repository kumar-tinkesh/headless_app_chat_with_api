iPhone User Guide
Find your password for a website or app on iPhone
You can find your password for a website or app by searching or sorting your accounts on iPhone.
Find your password for a website or app by searching for the account
Go to the Passwords app on your iPhone.
Enter the name of the account in the search field at the top of the screen.
Tap the account, then tap the Password field.
Find your password for a website or app by sorting your accounts
Go to the Passwords app on your iPhone.
Tap All.
Tap in the lower-left corner, then tap how you want to sort your accounts.
Locate and tap the account, then tap the Password field.
