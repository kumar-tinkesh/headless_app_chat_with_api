Play podcasts with CarPlay
Use CarPlay to play the podcasts that are on your iPhone.
You can use your vehicles’s built-in controls to open Podcasts and select a podcast to play.
Note: If you’re viewing CarPlay Dashboard, tap to see pages of all of your CarPlay apps, including Podcasts.
You can also use the controls on your vehicle’s steering wheel, the buttons on the Now Playing screen, and CarPlay Dashboard to control podcast playback.
