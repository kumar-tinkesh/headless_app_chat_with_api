iPhone User Guide
Read and bookmark the iPhone User Guide
When you view the iPhone User Guide in the Safari app, you can change the language and country or region, add a bookmark, or save it as a shortcut on the Home Screen. You can also view the guide in the Tips app.
View and bookmark the iPhone User Guide in Safari
Go to the Safari app on your iPhone.
Go to https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone .
To view the user guide in a different language, scroll down to the bottom of the page, tap the country or region link (United States, for example), then choose a country or region.
For quick access, tap , then choose any of the following:
Add to Home Screen: The shortcut appears as a new icon on the Home Screen.
Add Bookmark: The bookmark appears when you tap in Safari.
View the iPhone User Guide in Tips
Go to the Tips app on your iPhone.
Scroll down to User Guides, then tap iPhone.
