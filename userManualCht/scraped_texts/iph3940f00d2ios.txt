Use the Camera Control to open another app on iPhone
Instead of opening Camera, you can set the Camera Control ( on supported models ) to open the Magnifier app, the Code Scanner, or your favorite third-party camera app. You can also set the Camera Control to not open any app or feature when clicked.
Go to Settings , then tap Camera.
Tap Camera Control, then choose one of the following options:
Camera: Opens Camera.
Code Scanner: Opens a QR code scanner.
Magnifier: Opens the Magnifier app.
None: No app opens when you click the Camera Control.
