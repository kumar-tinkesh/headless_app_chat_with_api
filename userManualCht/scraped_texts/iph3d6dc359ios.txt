Share a recording in Voice Memos on iPhone
You can share one or more audio recordings with others (or send it to your Mac or another device) using AirDrop, Mail, Messages, and more.
Share a recording
Go to the Voice Memos app (in the Utilities folder) on your iPhone.
In the list of recordings, tap the recording you want to share, then tap .
Tap Share, choose a sharing option, select or enter a recipient, then tap Done or .
Share more than one recording
Go to the Voice Memos app (in the Utilities folder) on your iPhone.
Tap Edit above the list of recordings, then select the recordings you want to share.
Tap , choose a sharing option, select or enter a recipient, then tap or Done.
