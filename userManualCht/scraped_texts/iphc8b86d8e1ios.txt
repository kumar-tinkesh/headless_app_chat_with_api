iPhone User Guide
Listen to Apple News Today on iPhone
You can listen to Apple News Today, an audio briefing of the day’s top stories hosted by Apple News editors. With an Apple News+ subscription , you can also listen to audio versions of select Apple News+ stories in the News app and in the Podcasts app .
Note: Apple News, Apple News+, and the audio feature aren’t available in all countries or regions. See the iOS and iPadOS Feature Availability website .
Play Apple News Today
Go to the News app on your iPhone.
Tap , then tap an episode of Apple News Today.
Apple News Today briefings also appear in the Today feed below Listen Now.
For more audio controls, tap the mini player at the bottom to open the full-screen player.
See Listen to audio stories .
To return to the mini player, swipe down to minimize the full-screen player.
To close the full-screen player, tap , then tap Close Audio Player. To close the mini player, tap .
If you’re not an Apple News+ subscriber, a preview of an Apple News+ audio story plays when the briefing concludes.
Get Apple News Today show notes notifications
Go to the News app on your iPhone.
Tap Following, swipe to the bottom of the screen, then tap Notifications & Email below Manage.
Turn on notifications for Apple News Today.
