Annotate and save a webpage as a PDF in Safari on iPhone
You can mark up a webpage, highlight your favorite parts, draw and write notes, and share your document as a PDF with others.
Go to the Safari app on your iPhone.
Tap .
Tap Markup , then use the tools to annotate the webpage.
Tap Done, then tap Save File To.
Choose a file to save it to, then tap Save.
