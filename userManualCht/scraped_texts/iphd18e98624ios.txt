Use passwords on iPhone
In the Passwords app , you can find all your passwords, passkeys, and verification codes in one place. You can use them on all your devices where you sign in to the same AppleÂ Account and turn on Passwords & Keychain in iCloud settings. And if you use AutoFill to sign in to websites and apps, your passwords automatically appear in Passwords.

Tip: In Passwords, tap Security for recommendations to change weak passwords .
